About VG Forum

What is VG Forum?

VG Forum is a community tool designed to document and review video game development versions. Developers can share early builds of their games while players can track progress and provide feedback on different versions of a game.

Purpose of the Site

Many games go through dozens or even hundreds of updates during development. VG Forum allows players and developers to record changes, review gameplay progress, and track how a game evolves from early development stages to release.

What You Can Do Here

  • Browse all submitted game versions
  • Create a new version review
  • Search reviews by version or keywords
  • Track development progress

Understanding Completion Status

Each review can include a completion status that indicates how far the reviewer progressed in the game. This helps readers understand the perspective of the review.

  • ND No Dungeon reached
  • FD First Dungeon completed
  • SD Second Dungeon completed
  • TD Third Dungeon completed

Why Version Tracking Matters

Version tracking helps developers see how their game evolves over time. It also helps players understand what features were added, changed, or improved between versions.
